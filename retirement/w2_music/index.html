2. Music Frequencies

Calculation of music note frequencies and bank interest

This ariticle is a retirement project of my friend Charles. It explains today's piano tuning system, The Equal Temperament.


Note: Violin string tuning is slightly different with the following frequencies.

  E5: 660  (=440 * 3/2), vs 659.26 in piano
  A4: 440  (same as piano)
  D4: 293.33  (=440 * 2/3), vs 293.66 in piano
  G3: 195.55  (=440 * 2/3 * 2/3), vs 196 in piano

That's why violin players always feel the piano is a little out of tune. (It actually is, by design)   But pianists are more sophisticated in math - they know logarithm, while violinists can only do simple arithmetics :)
